Victoria - Equestrian City

SURFACE

It has an area of 252 km2 and has the geometric shape of a rectangle.

POPULATION

Victoria has more than 100,000 inhabitants. the urban zone concentrates the majority of inhabitants. We have a young population as 54% of the inhabitants are between the ages of 0 and 29.

CLIMATE

Its climate has two seasons: one dry and one rainy, which last about six months each. Its temperature is temperate and oscillates between 86° and 95° F degrees in winter and 72° to 77° F degrees in summer.

Our rodeos are nationally recognized as one of the best for their quality and organization, which is why we plan them several months before they are prepared.

Several riders from various farms on the Ecuadorian coast participate and exhibit complex skills such as the conch and the pial lasso, which are the most traditional.

The parks near the river that borders the city are one of the most visited places by children and their parents. Some couples contemplate the moon reflected in the calm waters. It is a place for all likes and to take photos.

Victoria is the land of quality rice by tradition. About 70% of the territory is suitable for the development of grass cultivation, for this reason, its production is of great importance in rural areas.

Victrorian rice is pearly in color and naturally aging. The land where it is planted is rich in nutrients and organic matter.

Twice a year it is harvested: in winter and in summer.

Rice processing is carried out in mills established in the canton, many of them with high technology.

Victoria is a city surrounded by rivers, so people go there on weekends to swim and have a good time with family and friends.

The Babahoyo River is the main source of water for the city.

Victoria has the only racetrack in Ecuador. It is called Miguel Salem Divo, the race track is 1,600 meters long and 22 meters wide where every Sunday between 8 and 9 horse races take place.

It has 6,018 m2 of stables, a warm-up area and stables with all the services required for the good maintenance of the horses. Its facilities are functional, comfortable and have the technology that the fans demand. The private boxes are renovated and the betting system is computerized.

The traditional food of the Ecuadorian coast is highly desired by national and foreign tourists. Local cattle are treated to be of the best quality and their flavor can be distinguished in our dishes.

The rice with menestra, roast beef and patacones (made with fried plantains) is very exquisite to enjoy at any time.
